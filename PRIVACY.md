# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 11, 2025**

## Overview

NATO Phonetics is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed with a privacy-first approach and processes all data locally on your device.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

The app operates entirely offline and does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track user behavior
- Send analytics to third parties
- Require account creation
- Access your contacts, location, or other sensitive data

## Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

### 1. Theme Preference
- **What**: Your dark mode/light mode preference
- **Why**: To remember your theme choice between app sessions
- **Where**: Device local storage only
- **Can be deleted**: Yes, by clearing app data

### 2. Voice Selection
- **What**: Your preferred text-to-speech voice identifier
- **Why**: To use your selected voice for audio playback
- **Where**: Device local storage only
- **Can be deleted**: Yes, by clearing app data

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

### Text-to-Speech (Required)
- **Purpose**: To pronounce NATO phonetic words
- **Data Access**: Uses device's built-in speech synthesis
- **Data Shared**: None - all processing is on-device

## Third-Party Services

This app does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Device Features Used

- **Speech Synthesis**: Native device text-to-speech engine (no data leaves your device)
- **Local Storage**: AsyncStorage for preferences (stored only on your device)

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children. The app is safe for all ages as it processes all data locally and does not communicate with external servers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Data Deletion

To delete all app data:
- **iOS**: Settings > General > iPhone Storage > NATO Phonetics > Delete App
- **Android**: Settings > Apps > NATO Phonetics > Storage > Clear Data

## Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data to:
- Request access to

- Request deletion of
- Request portability of

All your data stays on your device and is under your complete control.
